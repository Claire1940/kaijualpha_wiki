<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Abilities and Skill Breakdown">

The combat loop for **kaiju alpha biollante** revolves around her ability to manipulate the battlefield. Her moveset is designed to keep enemies at bay while dealing consistent damage through both direct strikes and environmental hazards. Her primary strength lies in her tendrils, which offer a reach that few other melee units can match.

| Ability Name | Cooldown | Damage Output | Type | Key Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Four Horsemen** | 94s | 350 | Custom | Summons 4 NPCs (1000 HP each) |
| **Liaan Purgation** | 3s | 400 - 676 | Melee | Multi-hit tendril slam |
| **Liaan Rapture** | 4s | 450 - 750 | Melee | Lunge and snap attack |
| **Acid Swamp** | Variable | High DOT | Area | Zone denial and heavy damage |
| **Cellular Rush** | Variable | Gap Closer | Mobility | First ability gained without rework |

### The Power of the Four Horsemen
This is the defining trait of the **kaiju alpha biollante**. By digging her tendrils into the earth, she summons four additional tendrils that act as stationary guardians. Each tendril lasts for a full minute, providing a massive buffer zone. Enemies attempting to reach the main body must either destroy these 1000-HP NPCs or take constant chip damage while navigating the terrain.

<Callout type="warning" title="Cooldown Management">
The Four Horsemen has a long 94-second cooldown. Always ensure you are in a strategic location, such as a narrow pass or a capture point, before deploying them to maximize their 60-second lifespan.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Combat Mastery">

To play **kaiju alpha biollante** effectively, you must transition from a reactive mindset to a proactive one. You do not wait for the fight to come to you; you prepare the ground so that the fight is won before it even begins.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Territory Selection">
    Identify a high-traffic area or a defensive objective. Locations like Outpost Bravo or the narrow corridors of Tokyo are ideal for Biollante's wide-reaching attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="NPC Deployment">
    Activate **The Four Horsemen** immediately upon entering a zone you intend to hold. Position yourself in the center of your summoned tendrils to create a "safe zone."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Zone Denial">
    Layer your **Acid Swamp** over the areas where enemies are likely to approach. This forces them to either take damage over time or take a longer route through your tendrils.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Micro-Management">
    Use **Immortal Suffering** whenever you take a significant hit. The +450 HP boost is short-lived but can negate the impact of a heavy melee strike or a partial beam hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execution">
    Once an enemy is weakened by your NPCs and Acid, use **Liaan Rapture** for its high burst damage to finish them off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Range Advantage">
Your tendrils offer significantly more range than standard kaiju claws. Always keep a few meters of distance; if an enemy gets close enough to touch your main body, you have lost your primary advantage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Historical Evolution and Trivia">

The **kaiju alpha biollante** has a storied history within the game. She held the record for the longest time without a remodel—remaining in her original state from the game's release until the major update in January 2024. This remodel not only updated her visuals but also refined her "proper swim" animations, moving away from the old "run-walk" variation used underwater.

### Key Historical Milestones
- **October 2023**: Temporarily went off-sale alongside other Heisei-era icons.
- **January 2024**: Received a massive visual and mechanical overhaul, increasing her price and effectiveness.
- **Firsts**: She was the first kaiju to leave trails when moving and the first to utilize NPC summons.

| Milestone | Date | Impact |
