<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Skull Location Route">

Finding every skull requires visiting specific landmarks. Because the map features massive vertical scale, using a ground-bound behemoth will make this hunt incredibly slow. We highly recommend unlocking a fast, flying, or swimming kaiju before attempting this route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure a High-Mobility Kaiju">
    Select a character with flying capabilities or high movement speed. Showa Jet Jaguar is an exceptional choice due to its flight mechanics and quick repositioning tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sweep the Tokyo Landmarks">
    Begin in Tokyo. Search the Tokyo Reactor area, check the shipyard docks, and dive into the deep underwater chasm located directly behind the city shoreline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Search the Extreme Biomes">
    Travel to Antarctica and navigate deep inside the ice caves. Once secured, head to Skull Island and search the perimeter of the central lake and surrounding cliffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim the Final Skulls in Hong Kong">
    Fly to Hong Kong and search the high mountain peaks. Inspect the narrow crevices and rear sides of major rock formations where skulls are tucked out of normal sightlines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Activate the Unlock Screen">
    Once you have collected all 10 unique skulls, open the main kaiju roster menu. Select the Monster Zero entry and click the claim button to permanently add him to your selection screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>
