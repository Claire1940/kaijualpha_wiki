<Section icon={Swords} title="Complete Move Analysis & Axe Mechanics">

Kong 2021 features a versatile moveset that changes dynamically depending on whether his Battle Axe is drawn. Managing your energy levels to pull out the axe is the core gameplay loop for high-level players.

### Base Moveset (Unarmed)
Without his axe, Kong relies on heavy physical strikes, ground pounds, and self-preservation skills:

| Move Slot | Action Name | Damage / Effect | Tactical Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Move 1 | Right Hand Strike | Low-Moderate | Quick poke to interrupt enemy startups |
| Move 2 | Ground Slam | Moderate | Good area-of-effect strike for groups |
| Move 3 | Rejuvenation | Heals 300 HP | Restores health; highly vulnerable during cast |
| Move 4 | Multi-Slam | High | Unleashes a rapid flurry of ground slams |
| Move 5 | Jump Slam | Very High (570+ base) | Heavy closing strike with excellent range |

### Battle Axe Moveset (Utility Active)
Once your energy meter reaches 100, activating your utility pulls the Battle Axe from the ground. This completely alters your combat effectiveness:

| Move Slot | Axe Action | Damage / Effect | Tactical Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Move 3 (Axe) | Shield Block | Deflects damage | Absorbs attacks and returns damage to attackers |
| Move 5 (Axe) | Jump Axe Slam | Extreme (800 - 1,300+) | Devastating overhead leap that crushes targets |
| Utility | Throw Axe | Discards weapon | Resets moveset back to unarmed status |

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Stat Allocation Guide">

When leveling your Kong 2021 to the maximum level of 100, dumping all your points into raw strength is highly inefficient. Because Kong's scaling benefits heavily from survivability, a hybrid build is required to survive chaotic free-for-all lobbies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Max Out Health (HP)">
    Allocate your points to bring your maximum HP up to the **5,500 cap**. This provides the massive health pool needed to survive heavy bursts from high-level opponents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Split Points into Rejuvenation">
    Invest roughly **50% of your remaining points** into Rejuvenation. This increases how quickly your energy and health recover, allowing you to build back to 100 energy rapidly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Allocate the Rest to Strength">
    Place the final **50% of points** into Strength. Since the Battle Axe base damage is already exceptionally high, you do not need maxed strength to easily defeat opponents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Build Type | HP Allocation | Strength Allocation | Regen Allocation | Playstyle |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Hybrid Meta** | Maxed (5,500) | 50% Remaining | 50% Remaining | High survival, fast energy, massive axe burst |
| Glass Cannon | Low | Maxed | Low | Extremely risky, easily bursted down by groups |
| Tank Build | Maxed (5,500) | Low | Maxed | High survival, slow kill times, low burst |

<Callout type="success" title="The Regen Advantage">
With a 50% investment in Rejuvenation, your energy returns to full almost instantly after spending it, allowing you to keep your Battle Axe active for consecutive fights.
</Callout>
