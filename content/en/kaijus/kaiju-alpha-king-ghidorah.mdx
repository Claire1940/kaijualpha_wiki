### The Voltage Charging System

Unlike standard titans, your ultimate move (Thunderstorm) cannot be spammed. You must hold your charge key to build up your voltage meter. It builds continuously at a rate of 3 to 5 points per second.

  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Damage Scaling">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Regeneration">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boost Max Health">
    Add points to your Health pool to reach the 6,000 HP threshold. This provides the necessary cushion to survive surprise attacks from heavy hitters like Space Godzilla or Thermonuclear variants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Balance Speed and Utility">
    Distribute your remaining points into Speed. Avoid over-investing in speed if you run damage-over-time builds, as excessive speed points can occasionally reduce your damage ticks per second on sustained beams.
  </Step>
</Steps>
