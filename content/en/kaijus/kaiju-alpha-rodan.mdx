The core mechanic of the kaiju alpha rodan playstyle revolves around the orange Canned Heat bar. By landing specific attacks, you generate heat resources. Once your bar is full, activating your **Heat Riser** utility on the ground boosts your overall damage output and passive health regeneration.

This system features a decaying loop. When the temporary Stage 4 duration expires, your power level drops back to Stage 3. This allows skilled players to quickly cycle back into Stage 4 if they manage their Canned Heat efficiently during combat.

| Heat Stage | Visual Indicators | Damage Buff | Unique Ability |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Stage 1** | Default model appearance | None | Standard Roar |
| **Stage 2** | Slight body glow | Minor | Standard Roar |
| **Stage 3** | Lava dripping from mouth | Moderate | Standard Roar |
| **Stage 4** | Pulsating red wing cracks | Massive | Flaming Demon Rage AoE |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy and Ability Combos">

Titanus Rodan excels at punishing slower opponents by abusing the animation delays on his primary moves. His kit features two primary high-impact abilities that can turn the tide of any battle.

| Ability Name | Cooldown | Damage Value | Energy Cost | Attack Type |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Flaming Demon Rage** | 10 Seconds | 1,200 (Stage 4) | 35 Charge / 15 Heat | Roar AoE |
| **Flaming Demon Summon** | 10 Seconds | None | None | Utility Roar |

To execute his most effective combat loop, players should focus on utilizing *Vulcan Burst* and *Ragnarok*. These moves feature wide hitboxes and high base damage, making them incredibly difficult for opponents to punish.
