<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Capabilities and Symbiosis">

In battle, the **Kaiju Alpha Titan X** utilizes a combination of overwhelming physical strength and specialized symbiotic support. Its primary weapons are its six massive tentacles, which are strong enough to drag Monarch patrol vessels underwater with zero resistance. During the 2017 Skull Island encounter, the Titan demonstrated its ability to trade blows with Kong, even managing to draw blood from the Great Ape's mouth using its serrated hooks.

**The Scarab Symbiosis:**
The relationship between Titan X and the Scarabs is one of the most sophisticated examples of mutualism in the Monsterverse. These smaller amphibious creatures reside on the Titan's body, acting as a mobile defense force and maintenance crew.

<Section icon={Target} title="Battle History and Key Encounters">

The historical record of the **Kaiju Alpha Titan X** is marked by high-stakes encounters with both humanity and fellow Titans. While generally peaceful and driven by migratory instincts, the Titan X becomes a devastating force when its migration path is obstructed or its offspring are threatened.

**1957 Santa Soledad Incident:**
The Titan first appeared to Lee Shaw and Keiko Randa. Initially aggressive due to the presence of dormant Scarabs, it quickly pacified once the symbionts were safely in the water. This established the Titan's role as a protector rather than a mindless destroyer.

**2017 Axis Mundi Emergence:**
Following a premature awakening caused by a Suzuki device, Titan X emerged into the surface world via the Skull Island rift. This led to a series of confrontations:
1. **Vs. Kong**: A brutal stalemate in the waters of Skull Island where the Titan X used its superior aquatic mobility to evade capture.
2. **Vs. Apex Cybernetics**: A failed attempt by Isabel Simmons to control the Titan using neural implants, which was thwarted by the creature's sheer willpower and motherly instincts.
3. **Vs. Godzilla**: A brief but intense standoff where the Titan X managed to wound the King of the Monsters before retreating to protect its egg.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Weaknesses and Containment Protocols">

Despite its status as a high-tier predator, the **Kaiju Alpha Titan X** has distinct vulnerabilities that have been exploited by organizations like Apex Cybernetics. Its primary weakness is its overwhelming protective instinct toward its egg, which can be used to lure the Titan into traps.

**Primary Vulnerabilities:**
- **Anesthetic Sensitivity**: Large concentrations of specialized anesthetic gas can render the Titan unconscious, as demonstrated on Skull Island.
- **Neural Disorientation**: While it can resist direct control, high-frequency neural implants can scramble the Titan's internal navigation, throwing it off its migratory route.
- **Subsonic Interference**: Artificial sonic calls can be used to misdirect the Titan, though this is risky as it often incurs the creature's wrath once the deception is discovered.
