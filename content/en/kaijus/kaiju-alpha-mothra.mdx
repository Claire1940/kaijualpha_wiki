<Section icon={Swords} title="Active Abilities & Combat Mechanics">

The combat loop of the Mothra Larva 1964 centers on a simple yet highly effective two-button kit. While other kaiju rely on complex combo systems, this character focuses on landing precise skill shots and executing hit-and-run tactics. Understanding the range, cooldown, and utility of each move is essential for survival.

| Ability Key | Ability Name | Attack Type | Cooldown | Primary Function |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Key 1 | Ground Bash | Physical | Short | Close-range defense and knockback |
| Key 2 | Silk String | Beam | Medium | Aim-able ranged slow and damage |

The Ground Bash serves as your primary defensive tool. When an enemy gets too close, slamming the ground creates a small shockwave that pushes opponents back, giving you enough time to retreat. The Silk String is your signature move. It is the only aim-able beam among all the starter characters, allowing you to lead your shots and hit moving targets with high precision.

<Callout type="tip" title="Aiming Technique">
Since the silk beam is manually aim-able, use your camera controls to predict enemy movement. Aim slightly ahead of running opponents to guarantee the slow effect lands.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="History, Trivia & FAQ">

The Mothra Larva 1964 has a unique place in the development history of the game. Originally, it was not a free starter character, requiring players to spend U-cells to unlock it. Over time, balance changes and progression updates reshaped its role, turning it into the entry-level utility character players know today.

- **Historical Cost**: At one point in development, this was the cheapest purchasable kaiju, costing 4,500 U-cells before being converted into a free starter.
- **Unique Mechanics**: It remains the only starting character in the game that features an aim-able projectile beam.
- **Cinematic Reference**: The design is based directly on the 1964 Showa film, where twin larvae successfully defeat Godzilla by trapping him in a cocoon of silk.
