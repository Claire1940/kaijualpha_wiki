<Section icon={Zap} title="Abilities and Move-set Analysis">

The toolkit of the **Kaiju Alpha Burning Godzilla** is built around the "Infinite Heat Ray" and "Nuclear Pulse." Mastering the rotation between these two is the difference between a novice and a veteran player. In 2026, the developers adjusted the cooldowns to favor players who can cycle their utility skills effectively.

| Ability Name | Cooldown | Damage Type | Effect |
| | | | |
| Crimson Claw | 2.0s | Physical | Applies Burn DoT for 5 seconds |
| Spiral Heat Ray | 8.0s | Energy | High-penetration beam with knockback |
| Nuclear Pulse | 12.0s | AoE | Clears nearby projectiles and stuns |
| Infinite Heat Ray | 15.0s | Ultimate | Massive beam that grows wider over time |
| Meltdown Utility | 1.0s | Buff/Debuff | Toggles damage boost and HP drain |

The **Nuclear Pulse** is your primary defensive tool. While Burning Godzilla lacks a traditional shield, the pulse can negate incoming missile barrages and push back aggressive melee flyers like Rodan or Mothra.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Activating the Meltdown utility while under 15% HP is almost always fatal. Use the "Successive Regen" build if you plan on staying in Meltdown state for extended periods.
</Callout>

**Ability Rotation Strategy:**
1. Initiate with **Spiral Heat Ray** to soften the target.
2. Toggle **Meltdown** once the distance is closed.
3. Use **Crimson Claw** twice to stack the Burn debuff.
4. Execute **Nuclear Pulse** if the enemy attempts to counter-attack.
5. Finish with **Infinite Heat Ray** before toggling Meltdown off to recover.
