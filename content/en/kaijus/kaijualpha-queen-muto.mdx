<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Tactics and Matchup Guide">

The **kaijualpha queen muto** is a melee specialist. Unlike many other titans in the game, she does not possess a traditional long-range beam. This means your positioning is the most important factor in every fight. 

**Combat Strategy vs. Different Tiers:**

| Enemy Type | Difficulty | Recommended Strategy |
|------------|------------|----------------------|
| **Godzilla (Standard)** | Medium | Bait the beam, then use speed to close in and Stomp. |
| **Kraken** | Low/Medium | Stay inside its hitbox to avoid tentacle swipes. |
| **Shimo** | High | Avoid the frost breath at all costs; only attack from the rear. |
| **Monster X** | Extreme | Do not engage solo unless you have a significant level advantage. |
| **Femuto** | Very Low | One or two Stomps will usually clear them. |

The **Big Stomp** is your bread and butter. It deals roughly 975 damage when strength is maxed. However, it has a physical contact requirement. If you hit the ground but the enemy is 50 feet away, they will take zero damage. This "prison rules" style of combat requires you to be aggressive and stay on top of your target.
