<Section icon={Sparkles} title="U-Cell Farming and Leveling Strategy">

Progressing to the endgame of **kaijualpha 1.0** requires millions of U-cells and thousands of experience points. To reach the Level 200 cap efficiently, you must move away from random destruction and adopt a structured farming loop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select your Farmer">
    Choose Biollante or Godzilla 2019. These kaiju have wide-area attacks that can hit 4-5 buildings simultaneously, maximizing U-cell gain per cooldown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate High-Density Zones">
    Head to the Tokyo Reactor or the central Hong Kong district. These areas feature the tightest clusters of destructible assets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Skill Rotation">
    Do not spam all buttons. Use your widest area-of-effect (AoE) skill first, then use single-target skills to clean up remaining structures while the main AoE recharges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The 50% Rule">
    When a city block is 50% destroyed, move to the next one. Waiting for the final few buildings to collapse is less efficient than hitting a fresh, dense cluster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Utilize Private Servers">
    If possible, farm in low-population or private servers. This prevents other players from interrupting your loop or "stealing" your building clusters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Farming Zone | Building Density | Risk Level | Recommended Kaiju |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tokyo Reactor** | Very High | High (PvP Hotspot) | Biollante |
| **Hong Kong Bay** | High | Medium | Godzilla 2019 |
| **Skull Island** | Medium | Low | Suko / Junior |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always keep an eye on your XP bar. If you are close to a level-up, prioritize finishing buildings over engaging in a fight. Leveling up refreshes your health and stamina, which can save you if a hostile player suddenly attacks.
</Callout>

To unlock Monster Zero, you must collect all 10 skulls in a single session or across multiple sessions (check your progression menu to see which are saved). It is highly recommended to use a flying kaiju like **Showa Jet Jaguar** or a fast swimmer like **Godzilla Aquatilis** to reach these distant landmarks quickly.
