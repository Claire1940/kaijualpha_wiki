<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Controls and Input Layout">

To survive against massive opponents, you must memorize your monster's standard layout. Every beast in the game utilizes five distinct skill keys and two roar buttons. Managing these cooldowns while maintaining dynamic movement is the difference between winning a duel and getting pinned against the city skyline.

### Default PC Keybinds

| Input Key | Action Performed | Optimal Combat Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **W / A / S / D** | Basic Movement | Circle strafing and dodging heavy incoming beams |
| **Mouse** | Camera & Aim | Tracking high-speed flying or swimming targets |
| **Keys 1 - 3** | Low-Cooldown Skills | Constant pressure, quick physical pokes, and combos |
| **Keys 4 - 5** | Signature / Ultimate | High-damage finishers; use when enemies are animation-locked |
| **Key R** | Roar 1 | Offensive space creation or utility buffs |
| **Key T** | Roar 2 | Defensive pushback or secondary crowd control |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Warning">
Never deplete your stamina bar completely. If you run out of stamina during an aggressive combo, you will be unable to dash or reposition, leaving you completely vulnerable to high-damage atomic blasts.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Starter Progression Strategy">

Starting your journey in a chaotic monster playground can be daunting. Choosing the right starter and building a solid foundation of U-cells is critical to unlocking late-game powerhouses. Follow this step-by-step path to establish your presence on the server.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Forgiving Starter">
    Begin with Kamata-Kun or Godzilla Junior. Kamata-Kun is highly recommended because its ranged pressure allows you to attack from a safe distance, keeping you away from chaotic melee scrums.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Skill Rotations">
    Test your selected kaiju's skills in isolated areas first. Learn the exact reach, animation speed, and recovery times of your 1–5 keys before engaging in active PvP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Focus on Building Destruction">
    Do not hunt players immediately. Target high-density city blocks and reactor zones. Destroying structures provides a steady, risk-free stream of experience and Cells.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save Cells for a Specific Goal">
    Avoid buying multiple low-tier monsters. Keep your resources focused on unlocking a specialized farmer like Biollante or a top-tier PvP duelist like Final Wars Godzilla.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Efficiency">
Farming structures is significantly faster when you align yourself with the center of a building cluster. This ensures your wide-area attacks hit multiple targets simultaneously, maximizing your skill value.
</Callout>

</Section>
