<Callout type="warning" title="Prerequisite Check">

<Steps>

  <Step num="2" title="Reach Level 100">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Accumulate U-Cells">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Transformation">

The combat loop for the **kaijualpha orga second form** revolves around its five numbered skill slots and two roar inputs. Orga is unique because its Skills 4 and 5 have longer wind-up times but can effectively one-shot smaller kaiju if they connect fully.

1. Open with **T (Roar)** to slow the target.
2. Use **2 (Leap)** to engage.

4. If the enemy is low on stamina, charge **4 (Wave Cannon)** for the kill.
