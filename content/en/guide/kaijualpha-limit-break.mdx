<Section icon={Shield} title="Costs and Unlocking Methods">

Unlocking the **kaijualpha limit break** is a major milestone that requires significant resources. The developers have provided two primary paths to access this system: the F2P (Free to Play) path using Cells and the Premium path using Robux. 

| Method | Resource Type | Estimated Cost | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Grinding** | Cells | 50,000 - 100,000 | Active daily players |
| **Instant Unlock** | Robux | 500 - 800 | Time-limited players |
| **Event Reward** | Special Tokens | Seasonal | Completionists |

Cells are earned by defeating other Kaijus, completing daily missions, and participating in world events. While the Cell cost is high, it is achievable through consistent gameplay. The Robux option is intended for players who wish to skip the resource gathering phase and immediately begin their journey toward level 200.

<Callout type="info" title="Resource Management">
If you are choosing the Cell path, it is recommended to save your currency starting from level 80. By the time you hit level 100, you should have a substantial portion of the required Cells ready for the upgrade.
</Callout>

The unlock is permanent for that specific Kaiju. If you switch to a different Kaiju, you will need to unlock the limit break for the new character separately. This encourages players to master a single "Main" Kaiju before branching out to others.
