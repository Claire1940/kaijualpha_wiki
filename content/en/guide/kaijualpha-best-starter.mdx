### Heisei Godzilla
Heisei Godzilla is widely regarded by experienced players as the optimal choice for cell farming. The main advantage of this character lies in its dual-beam kit. By positioning yourself on high ground, such as a mountain side or a tall structure, you can fire both beams down into crowded combat zones. This allows you to tag dozens of active players and AI enemies without putting yourself in direct danger.

### Shin Godzilla
Shin Godzilla is a powerhouse suited for close-to-medium range skirmishes. While it lacks the extreme range of Heisei, it compensates with devastating area-of-effect attacks. Its signature back beam is perfect for situations where you are surrounded by other players. To utilize Shin Godzilla effectively, look for crowded areas where players are teaming or clumping together, move into the center, and activate your back beam to hit everyone simultaneously.

### Base Godzilla
Base Godzilla is the standard entry point for new players. While it does not match the specialized farming speeds of Heisei or Shin, it features the Atomic Sky Attack, which provides a respectable area of effect. Using Base Godzilla is necessary for fulfilling the level requirements needed to purchase and unlock the more powerful variants.
