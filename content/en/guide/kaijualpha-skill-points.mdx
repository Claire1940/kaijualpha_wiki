<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Efficient Cell and EXP Farming Route">

To maximize your **kaijualpha skill points** (in the form of Cells and EXP), you must establish a repeatable farming loop. Efficiency is determined by "damage-per-second" against environmental objects. The goal is to minimize travel time between building clusters while keeping your skills on a constant rotation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select your Farmer">
    Choose a kaiju with wide-area attacks. **Biollante** is the gold standard for building destruction, but **Suko** or **Godzilla Junior** are excellent budget alternatives for newer players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate High-Density Zones">
    Target the **Tokyo Reactor** or **Hong Kong city center**. These areas feature the most buildings packed into the smallest radius, allowing one AoE skill to hit multiple structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Rotation">
    Lead with your largest AoE (usually Skill 3 or 4). While those recharge, use your light attacks (Skill 1) to clean up remaining debris. Move toward the next block before the last building falls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Avoid Distractions">
    In a farming session, PvP is a resource sink. If a stronger player initiates a fight, use your mobility or roars to disengage. Survival is key to maintaining your EXP-per-minute ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Level 200 Push">
    Once you reach the initial level cap, invest your accumulated Cells into the **Limit Break** system. This allows your farmer to continue gaining levels, further increasing your prestige and power.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Limit Break system is where veteran players separate themselves from the crowd. This requires a significant Cell investment but is necessary to reach the prestigious **Level 200**.

Limit Breaking a kaiju doesn't just provide a gold-bordered level; it signifies that the character has reached its peak potential. In the 2026 competitive scene, most top-ranked players only use Limit Broken kaiju for serious clan wars and territory control.
