<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: How to Trigger and Win a Beam Clash">

Initiating a **kaijualpha beam clash** requires precise timing, proper positioning, and rapid execution. Follow this structured progression to consistently dominate energy struggles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Align Your Target">
    Position your kaiju on level ground directly facing your opponent. Standard beam skills travel in straight lines, so keeping your crosshair centered on the enemy's torso is critical for alignment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait the Enemy Beam">
    Wait for the opponent to initiate their ranged skill. Watch for visual tells, such as glowing dorsal plates or charge-up particles, before activating your own beam to collide with theirs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Button Mash">
    Once the beams connect, the clash interface will appear on your screen. Immediately begin rapidly tapping your primary attack or interaction keys to push the energy boundary toward the enemy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Follow-Up Stun">
    Upon winning the struggle, the opponent will be knocked back and stunned. Use this brief window to close the distance and execute a high-damage physical combo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
If you misalign your shot, your beam will miss the collision zone entirely, leaving you locked in an animation while taking full damage from the enemy's attack.
</Callout>
