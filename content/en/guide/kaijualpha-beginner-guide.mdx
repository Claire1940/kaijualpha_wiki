<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering Kaiju Alpha Controls and Mechanics">

Entering the world of Kaiju Alpha requires a solid understanding of the UI and the input system. Unlike traditional Roblox fighting games, Kaiju Alpha utilizes a structured ability bar where every monster has five distinct skills and two unique roars. Success in the early game depends on your ability to cycle these cooldowns while maintaining spatial awareness to avoid being trapped by larger, more aggressive players.

- **Moveset Overview**: A detailed look at the five-skill combat system.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="Zi3EukJHCzU" title="MONSTER X SHOWCASE! - Roblox Kaiju Alpha" />

The control scheme is standardized across the entire roster, allowing you to switch between monsters without relearning the basics. However, the "weight" and "wind-up" of attacks vary significantly between a small starter like Suko and a heavyweight like Final Wars Godzilla.

| Input | Action | Best Use Case | Combat Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 - 5** | Skill Activation | Primary combat and farming | Don't spam all five; save one for defense |
| **R** | Roar 1 | Utility or area knockback | Use to clear space when surrounded |
| **T** | Roar 2 | Secondary utility/buff | Check if your roar provides a damage boost |
| **W/A/S/D** | Movement | Positioning and dodging | Always strafe in circles during PvP |
| **Space** | Jump/Fly | Evading ground-based beams | Flying kaiju should stay high to scout |

<Callout type="tip" title="Stamina Management">
Every action, including basic movement and skill usage, consumes a portion of your stamina. Never let your bar hit zero during a fight, as it leaves you unable to dodge incoming atomic breaths or heavy physical slams.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Input Delay">
Some high-damage skills have a "cast time" or "wind-up." If you are hit during this animation, the skill may be canceled and put on cooldown without dealing damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Efficient U-Cell Farming Routes">

Farming in Kaiju Alpha is a science of maximizing "Destruction-per-Second" (DPS). Buildings are your primary source of both Experience (EXP) and U-cells. To progress quickly, you must establish a route that allows buildings to respawn by the time you complete a full circle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Tokyo Reactor">
    This area has the highest density of mid-sized buildings. Start here and use your AoE skills (usually Skill 3 or 4) to level entire blocks at once.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rotate to the Shipyards">
    Once the Reactor area is cleared, move toward the coast. The cranes and containers provide quick EXP and are often ignored by other players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Hong Kong Mountains">
    If the city is too crowded, head to the mountainous regions. While buildings are sparser, there is less risk of being interrupted by high-level PvP hunters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset and Repeat">
    By the time you finish the Hong Kong loop, the Tokyo Reactor buildings should have respawned. Return and begin the cycle again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Server Hopping">
If you find a server where a Level 200 player is camping the main city, don't hesitate to server hop. Farming is 300% more efficient in a quiet server where you don't have to worry about constant respawns.
</Callout>

| Farming Zone | Difficulty | Resource Yield | Recommended Kaiju |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Progression and Limit Break">

Once you reach the standard level cap, the real game begins. The **Limit Break** system allows you to push your kaiju to Level 200, unlocking massive stat boosts and prestige. This is a significant investment of U-cells and time, so choose your "Main" kaiju wisely before committing.

| Progression Step | Requirement | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Leveling** | 0 - 100 | Basic stat growth and skill unlocks |
| **Limit Break** | Level 100 + 50k Cells | Removes the level cap for that kaiju |
| **Post-Cap Grind** | Level 101 - 199 | Significant damage and health scaling |
| **Endgame Alpha** | Level 200 | Maximum power potential and aura |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Main">
    Pick a kaiju that you enjoy playing in both PvP and Farming. Final Wars Godzilla or Destoroyah are popular choices for Limit Breaking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase the Break">
    Go to the Evolution/Unlock menu and select the Limit Break option. This will consume a large amount of Cells but is necessary for endgame viability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Level 200 Push">
    Focus on "Alpha Fights"—engaging with other high-level players provides more EXP than buildings once you are past Level 150.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Priority">
When leveling past the cap, focus on Damage first. Being able to "one-shot" buildings with a single skill will accelerate the rest of your journey to Level 200.
</Callout>
